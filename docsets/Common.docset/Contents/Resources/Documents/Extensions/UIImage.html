  • AverageColorAlgorithm

    Algorithm for calculating average color.

    There are two main ways to get the color from an image: a simple “sum up an average” or by squaring their sums. Each has their advantages, but the ‘simple’ option seems better for average color of entire image and closely mirrors CoreImage. Details: https://sighack.com/post/averaging-rgb-colors-the-right-way
